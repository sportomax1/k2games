Jungle Swing: Vine Rush

Swing, release, and chain perfect arcs through an endless jungle run.

Desktop: Space/click grab-release, Arrow keys lean, P pause.
Mobile: Tap GRAB, lean buttons.

Tips

  • Grab a vine near the peak for a perfect swing combo.
  • Avoid thorns, logs, birds, and deep water.
  • Collect fruit crystals for bonus points and tiny speed burst.
  • Difficulty rises with distance.
