📖 Canyonlands Drift — Rules

🎯 Objective

Over 7 days at Lake Powell, collect resource cubes through activities, complete contracts for bonus points, and score the most adventure points to win!

🕐 Day & Hour System

Each day has 12 hours. Every activity costs a certain number of hours. When your hours run out, your day ends. The other player continues until their hours are also used up, then a new day begins.

Each player takes turns choosing one activity at a time. After all activities or when time runs out, the day ends.

⚡ Energy

You start each day with a maximum of 10 energy. Activities cost energy. If you don't have enough energy, you cannot perform an activity. Use Rest or Campfire to recover energy. When energy runs out you must rest or skip.

🎲 Push Your Luck

Some activities (Fishing, Jet Skiing, Cliff Jumping) use Push Your Luck: roll the die each turn. On success you collect cubes. You may Roll Again for more cubes or Take Cubes to stop safely. If you Bust (roll 1), you lose all cubes collected this activity!

📋 Contracts

5 contracts are active each game (drawn from 8 total). Complete them by having the required cubes — they are automatically fulfilled when you meet the requirements at the end of any action. Completed contracts score bonus points. Both players compete for the same contracts — first to meet requirements claims it!

🌤️ Weather

A weather card is revealed at the start of each day. Weather modifies activity outcomes for everyone that day:

  • ☀️ Sunny — +1 cube to all outdoor activities
  • 💨 Windy — Water push-your-luck has higher bust chance
  • ☁️ Overcast — No bonuses or penalties
  • 🌧️ Light Rain — Water activities give −1 cube (min 0)
  • ⛈️ Thunderstorm — Boating & Jet Ski unavailable
  • 🌫️ Fog — +1 Treasure chance on all activities
  • 🦅 Wildlife Sighting — Any activity also gives 1 Wildlife cube
  • 🔥 Heatwave — Hiking & Cliff costs +1 energy
  • 🌬️ Cool Breeze — All activities restore +1 energy
  • 🌙 Clear Night — Stargazing gives double Star cubes

🤝 Buddy System

Boating requires a buddy (worth +2 pts). In vs AI mode, the AI always counts as your buddy. In Pass & Play, the other player is your buddy for buddy-required activities. Buddy bonus is added automatically.

🏆 Scoring

  • 1 point per resource cube collected
  • Bonus points for completed contracts (see contract cards)
  • +2 points per buddy activity completed
  • Highest total score after 7 days wins!

🤖 vs Canyon AI

The AI analyzes which contracts are closest to completion and prioritizes activities that advance them. It uses moderate push-your-luck risk (stops after 2 successful rolls). AI takes its turn automatically after yours.

🤝 Pass & Play

After each player's turn, a cover screen appears prompting you to pass the device to the other player. Each player can only see their own strategy. Both players take turns each day until time runs out.
