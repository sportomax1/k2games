Play as a sheepdog in a glossy top-down pasture. Sweep around each flock, cut off escape routes, and funnel every sheep through the gate before the timer slips away. Each stage adds tighter fencing, longer funnels, and more stubborn herds.

Level 1 · Meadow Lesson Warm up on an open field and learn to steer the flock into the first pen.
Level 2 · Fence Funnel Narrow rails and staggered posts force you to shape the herd carefully.
Level 3 · Clover Switchbacks Guide the sheep around split paddocks and avoid scattering them into corners.
Level 4 · Sunset Stampede Final challenge: bigger flock, tighter time, and a long sprint home.
