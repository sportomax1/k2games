Switchyard Sentinel

Incoming trains ask for one of three stations: North Terminal, Central Depot, or South Freight. Use the Route Rules card while they approach: Switch A chooses the upper or lower branch; Switch B routes the upper branch to North or Center; Switch C routes the lower branch to South or Center.
