🧹 Scrub Squad
Clean It Before It Spreads
Rooms get dirty, biohazards erupt, and your cleaners can only move so fast. Click rooms to dispatch your squad — keep the infection meter down or the whole hospital goes under!

How to Play

  • Click a dirty room to dispatch the nearest cleaner
  • ☣️ Biohazard rooms — URGENT! They explode if ignored
  • Use 💦 Power Spray (bottom-right or Spacebar) to instantly clear a biohazard
  • Infection meter rises when rooms stay dirty — hit 100% = Game Over
  • Cleaners leave trails and spin while cleaning!
