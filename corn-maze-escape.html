Corn Maze Escape

You woke up inside a moonlit corn maze. The stalks are taller than houses, the rows all look alike, and the barn gate only opens after you collect three trail ribbons.

Desktop: W/S move, A/D strafe, arrow keys turn. Hold Shift to jog through long rows.
iPhone: left stick moves forward/back and strafes. Use the two turn buttons to look around.
Watch the compass, follow scarecrow arrows, and look for red ribbon posts glowing in the rows.
