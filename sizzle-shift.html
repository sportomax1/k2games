Sizzle Shift

Two tiny 3D chefs, one impossible kitchen. Prep ingredients, cook them, build exact plates, and launch meals through the service window before the tickets go cold.

Desktop: WASD or arrows to move. Space/E cooks, chops, grabs, plates, and serves.
Swap chefs with Tab/Q. Hold Shift for a quick dash through the lunch-rush fog.
iPhone: left thumbstick to move. Big Action, Dash, and Swap buttons stay near your thumbs.
