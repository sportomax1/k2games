# LAKE POWELL: LOST ADVENTURES
*A Competitive-Cooperative Worker Placement Adventure Game*

## 1. OVERVIEW

In *Lake Powell: Lost Adventures*, players are stranded on a houseboat and must make the most of their time exploring, surviving, and enjoying the lake. Balance **personal adventure points** with **shared survival resources** like fuel and food.

Use **worker placement**, **time management**, and **mini-game mechanics** tied to each activity to outscore your opponents—without stranding everyone.

---

## 2. COMPONENTS (PROTOTYPE FRIENDLY)

Use what you have:

- Colored cubes → Resources (Fuel, Food, Energy)
- Dice (black/white) → Random outcomes
- Playing cards → Event / Activity decks
- Chips → Player markers / scoring
- Paper → Board + Player mats

---

## 3. CORE CONCEPTS

### 🕒 Time System (MAIN MECHANIC)
- Each day = **12 hours**
- Players start at Hour 1
- Actions cost time (1–4 hours)
- When performing an activity, **skip forward that many hours**
- Players who finish earlier act again sooner

👉 This creates a **dynamic turn order system**

---

### ⚡ Energy System
- Each player starts with **10 Energy**
- Activities cost energy
- If you hit 0 → penalties
- Restore via:
  - Eating
  - Sleeping
  - Light activities

---

### ⛽ Shared Resources (CO-OP TENSION)
- Fuel and Food are **shared pools**
- Used for:
  - Boats
  - Jet skis
  - Group survival

If resources run out:
- ALL players suffer penalties

…but selfish play may still win 👀

---

### 👥 Buddy System
- Each player is paired with a “buddy”
- Activities requiring safety MUST be paired:
  - Boating
  - Skiing
  - Hiking

**Bonus Points:**
- +2 points per shared activity
- +5 if most activities completed together

---

## 4. GAME SETUP

1. Create board with:
   - Morning / Day / Night zones
   - Activity locations
   - Time track (1–12)
   - Shared resource pools

2. Each player gets:
   - 2 workers
   - 10 Energy
   - Starting position at Hour 1

3. Set shared:
   - Fuel = 20
   - Food = 20

4. Shuffle activity/event cards

---

## 5. TURN STRUCTURE

### On Your Turn:
1. Place a worker on an available location
2. Perform the action
3. Pay:
   - Time cost (move forward on track)
   - Energy cost
   - Resources (if required)

---

Oh this is a great design instinct—because pure randomness can feel unfair, but predictable systems feel strategic. What you want is “influenced randomness” (designer gold right there 🔥).

Here are some really strong ways to build weather into your game without it feeling chaotic:

🌤️ OPTION 1: Forecast Track (Best Starting Point)
How it works:
At the start of each day, reveal:

Today’s weather

Tomorrow’s weather (preview)

Example:
Day	Weather
Day 1	Sunny ☀️
Day 2	Windy 💨 (revealed early)
Why it works:
Players can plan ahead

Still uncertainty beyond 1 day

Feels thematic (like checking forecast)

🎲 OPTION 2: Weighted Weather Deck (Controlled Random)
Instead of equal randomness:

Create a deck like:

4x Sunny ☀️

3x Windy 💨

2x Cloudy ☁️

1x Storm ⛈

Twist:
Discard pile is visible → players can card count slightly

👉 So storms are rare, but not impossible

🧭 OPTION 3: Player Influence (SUPER FUN)
Let players slightly control weather:

Example mechanics:
Spend 2 energy → peek at next weather

Spend 3 food → swap tomorrow’s weather

Campfire action → stabilize weather

👉 Now weather becomes:

A resource decision

Not just RNG

⏳ OPTION 4: Time-of-Day Weather (VERY thematic)
Instead of 1 weather per day:

Time	Weather
Morning	Calm
Day	Windy
Night	Clear
Impact:
Jet ski = better morning

Boating = risky afternoon

Stargazing = depends on night

👉 This makes your time system even more meaningful

🌊 OPTION 5: Lake Conditions Track (My Favorite for Your Game)
Instead of “weather cards”… track lake conditions:

Track example:
Calm → Light Chop → Rough → Dangerous
How it changes:
Wind pushes it up

Calm weather brings it down

Player actions might affect it

Effects:
Condition	Impact
Calm	+1 to boating
Light Chop	Neutral
Rough	Jet ski risk ↑
Dangerous	Some actions blocked
👉 This ties DIRECTLY into your fuel + boating system

🎯 OPTION 6: Activity Modifiers (Simple + Effective)
Each weather gives modifiers:

☀️ Sunny
+1 to all activities

No penalties

💨 Windy
Jet ski risk ↑

Fishing harder

⛈ Storm
Boating CLOSED

Hiking risky

Campfire bonus

🌙 Clear Night
Stargazing bonus points

If you want a really strong system:

Use:
✅ Forecast Track (planning)
