Welcome, Controller. Your objective is to manage Terminal Airspace safely.

  • DEPARTURES: Clear them for takeoff. Once airborne, guide them out of the sector.
  • SEPARATION: Maintain at least 1000ft altitude difference or 50 units lateral distance.
  • CONTROLS: Click a plane to issue commands. Use sliders for vectoring.
