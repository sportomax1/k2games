Number Trap Rules

2-4 PLAYERS • 5 MIN

THE GOAL

Keep trap stability between 0 and 10. Force opponents to overload!

HOW TO PLAY

  • Start: Stability at 5
  • Players take turns playing 1 card
  • Announce new stability total
  • 🔵 Fuel (+) raises stability
  • 🔴 Coolant (-) lowers stability

OVERLOAD!

If stability goes outside 0 and 10, you overload! Take an error.

WIN/LOSE

3+ errors = you're out. Last survivor wins!

SPECIAL CARDS

Active only with 3-4 players!

= Copy: Repeat last card's change

🔄+4: Reverse order AND +4

±8 / ±7: Choose + or - value

-1🔄: -1 AND reverse order

IF ROUND ENDS

All players out of cards? Restart for higher stakes!
