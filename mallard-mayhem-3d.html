Mallard Mayhem

An original 3D-style prank-bird sandbox. Slip through a fancy park, annoy the humans, steal their stuff, and complete your whole list before they shoo you out for good.

Desktop: WASD or arrows to move, Shift to sprint, E to grab or interact, Space to honk.
Mobile: use the left stick to move, then Action, Honk, and Sprint on the right.
Steal the sandwich, dunk it in the fountain, ring the bell, snatch the groundskeeper hat, then escape through the gate.
