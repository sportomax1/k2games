🚑 Overtime ER
The Shift Never Ends
Patients arrive at the ER entrance. Click a patient to select them, then click an available room to assign them. Match severity to room type for bonus points. Don't let fatigue hit 100%!

How to Play

  • Click a patient in the waiting area to select
  • Click an available room (green glow) to assign
  • 🔴 Critical → Trauma rooms (double points)
  • 🟡 Urgent → Exam rooms (bonus points)
  • 🟢 Stable → Ward beds
  • Patients left waiting too long raise fatigue
