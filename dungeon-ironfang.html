Dungeon Ironfang

A first-person dungeon crawl in the old stone vault. Fight goblin guards, collect gems, find the iron key, and survive long enough to open the blue exit gate.

Desktop: W/S move, A/D strafe, arrow keys turn. Space or E swings your sword.
iPhone: left stick moves, turn buttons look around, and Attack swings when a monster is close.
Backpedal after you strike. Enemies hurt you when they crowd in, but gems and potions keep you alive.
