Checkout Lane Chaos
Items stream down the conveyor belt. Sort them into the right bins before they fall off — Frozen, Fragile, or Heavy. Grab coupons for bonus points. Don't let 3 items slip through!

How to Play

  • Items fall down the belt — watch for the glowing SORT ZONE
  • When an item enters the green zone, click its bin below!
  • 🧊 Frozen = bin [1] · 📦 Fragile = bin [2] · 🏋️ Heavy = bin [3]
  • Press 1 / 2 / 3 on keyboard for each bin
  • Catch ✂️ coupons for bonus · 3 missed = Game Over
