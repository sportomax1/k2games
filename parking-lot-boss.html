🚗 Parking Lot Boss
Pre-Shopping Showdown
Navigate the chaotic parking lot and grab a spot before someone else does. Dodge NPC cars and rogue shopping carts. The closer to the entrance, the bigger the score!

How to Play

  • Arrow Keys / WASD to drive
  • Reach a glowing open parking space 🟩
  • Avoid NPC cars (red) and shopping carts 🛒
  • Closer to entrance = higher score bonus
  • 3 crashes = Game Over
