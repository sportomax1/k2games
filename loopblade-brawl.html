Loopblade Brawl

Original snack warriors duel with returning loopblades in a toy-box arena. Throw, dash, catch, ricochet off bumpers, grab powerups, and win the round before the arena squeezes in.

Throwing sends your blade toward your aim. If it misses, call it back and catch it for a quick reload.
Dash through danger, slash when holding your blade, and use bumpers for sneaky bank shots.
iPhone controls: left stick moves, Throw auto-aims at the nearest rival, Dash dodges, Call recalls.
